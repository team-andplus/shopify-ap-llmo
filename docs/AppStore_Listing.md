# AI is the new search. Is your store ready?

ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and other AI assistants are crawling the web to answer user questions. When someone asks "What's a good sustainable fashion brand?", AI pulls information from websites it has crawled.

## Key Features

### 🤖 AI Crawler Analytics
See which AI bots visit your store in real-time.

- GPTBot (ChatGPT / OpenAI)
- PerplexityBot
- ClaudeBot (Anthropic)
- Google-Extended (Gemini)
- Amazonbot
- And 10+ more AI crawlers

Track visits by AI service, page, and date. Weekly email reports included.

### 📄 AI-Friendly Documents
Automatically generate documents that help AI understand your brand:

- **llms.txt** - Your store's identity, values, and key information for AI
- **.ai-context** - Rules and guidelines for how AI should interpret your store
- **llms.full.txt** - Complete catalog: products, collections, policies, locations
- **docs/ai/** - Custom markdown documents for AI reference

### ⚡ One-Click Installation
No code editing required. AP Visibility automatically:

- Adds AI document links to your store's <head>
- Sets up clean URLs (/llms.txt, /.ai-context)
- Generates AI sitemap (sitemap-ai.xml)

### 🧠 AI-Powered Generation
Use OpenAI to generate and refine your AI documents. Just describe your brand, and we'll create professional AI-ready content.

---

- **Track AI traffic**: See which AI systems are learning about your store.
- **Stay ahead**: Early adopters of AI-friendly search technologies will have a significant advantage.
